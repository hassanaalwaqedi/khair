🕌 Khair App — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Khair ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Khair mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information:

  • Name, email address, and password (encrypted)
  • Phone number (optional)

Usage Data:

  • Events you browse, join, or create
  • Search queries and category preferences
  • Language and theme preferences

Device Information:

  • Device type, operating system, and push notification token
  • Approximate location (only when granted permission, for nearby events)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Khair app services
  • To send push notifications about events and important updates
  • To personalize your event feed and recommendations
  • To communicate with organizers about events you join
  • To improve our services using anonymized, aggregated data

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data with:

  • Event organizers: your name when you join their event
  • Service providers: cloud hosting (Microsoft Azure), email delivery (SendGrid), AI services (Google Gemini) — strictly for app functionality
  • Legal requirements: if required by law

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored on secure Microsoft Azure servers
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms
  • All communication uses HTTPS encryption
  • JWT tokens expire after 24 hours

5. Location Data

We request location permission solely to show nearby events. Location data is not stored on our servers and is only used in real-time to filter events. You can deny location permission and still use the app.

6. Push Notifications

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send push notifications about event updates, admin announcements, and reminders. You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time.

7. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update your account information at any time
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out: Disable push notifications via device settings

8. Children's Privacy

Khair is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
